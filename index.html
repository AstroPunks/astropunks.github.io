About AstroPunks

AstroPunks is a collection of NFTs coming to Terra and the Lunatic community. Minting will go LIVE on Knowhere on March 1st!

NFTs for the Terraverse

Terra inspired punk astronauts. Put on your spacesuit and let's go intergalactic! The punks are specially designed for the Terraverse. With all kinds of different traits inspired by Terra, projects building on Terra and many more cool well-known attributes!

Made by Lunatics, for Lunatics!

Community is key. We want everyone to have a fair chance to hodl an AstroPunk. That's why we are going to airdrop 50 AstroPunks to community members! Just follow us on Twitter (@AstroPunks_NFT) for a chance to win.

Max. supply and price AstroPunks defined by you!

You have chosen and the results are in! The community has determined the max. supply and the price of the AstroPunks by voting on 2 Twitter polls. The maximum. supply has become 2500 AstroPunks, of which 50 will be airdropped to our Twitter followers. The remaining 2450 can be minted for only 25 $UST on knowhere.art on March 1st.
